FAQs

Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about Track 1on1 — from setup to the 11× guarantee.

Do I need to be a developer or technical person to use Track 1on1?

Not at all. You connect your accounts in one click and Track 1on1 deploys everything automatically. If you can log into Instagram, you can use Track 1on1.

How does Track 1on1 know which post made the sale?

The moment you connect your accounts, we attach a Smart Link to every post across every platform. When someone clicks, we follow that visitor through your page, video, and checkout — and tie the purchase back to the post that started it.

Will this slow down my website or sales page?

No. Track 1on1 runs silently in the background. Your page loads exactly the same speed. Your visitors never know it's there.

What platforms does Track 1on1 support?

Currently YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter (X). With full integrations for course platforms like Kajabi, Teachable, ThriveCart, Gumroad, and Stan Store — plus email tools like ConvertKit and ActiveCampaign.

How does the 11× guarantee actually work?

Simple. If at the end of your first month you can't see at least 11 times your subscription amount in attributed revenue inside your Track 1on1 dashboard — you pay nothing. No forms, no arguments, no fine print.

What exactly does the Auto DM feature do?

When someone comments on your reel, replies to your tweet, or uses a keyword you set — Track 1on1 automatically sends them a DM with your link or message instantly. You set it once, it runs 24/7.

Is my data and my audience's data safe?

Yes. Track 1on1 uses enterprise-grade encryption and never sells, shares, or exposes your audience data. Everything tracked stays in your dashboard and belongs to you.

What's the difference between Track 1on1 and just using UTM links?

UTM links tell you someone clicked. Track 1on1 tells you who clicked, which video minute made them click, how long they stayed, how much of your VSL they watched, and exactly how much money that one post made you.

Can I track competitors even if I don't follow them?

Yes. Just type in any YouTube channel, Instagram handle, or Twitter account. Track 1on1 scans their top-performing content publicly and generates outlines for you — no following needed.

How quickly can I get set up and see my first data?

Most creators see their first tracked clicks within minutes of connecting. Full revenue attribution typically shows up within 24 to 48 hours depending on your traffic volume.

Which apps does Track 1on1 integrate with?

Whop, Stripe, YouTube, Twitter (X), Instagram, Skool, Telegram, Cloudflare, Calendly, Close CRM, iClosed, HubSpot, WebinarJam, AEvent, GoHighLevel, Teachable, Fanbasis, ClickFunnels, Framer, Kit, Cal, Tally, Typeform — and more added monthly.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime — no hidden fees, no contracts, no friction.
